F.A.Q.
The following are some of the more frequently asked questions.

How long does it take?

Depends, usually no more than 10 hours. We run over 16 utilities to get your PC running at its very best. Some of these tools run very deep scans to ensure that all malicious software has been removed, some of these scans can take a while. We stay connected to your system during the entire process.

What do I have to do?

Not much. Your technician will guide you through simple process of getting us connected to your PC. Once connected you just sit back and watch us do our thing. You have the choice whether you want to stay on the line, have us call you back, or comminicate using the online chat option. You may be conntacted during the clean-up process to launch the remote access utility in the eventy that we get disconencted.


Do you look at my stuff?

No way! We do not peek around in your pictures, videos, or any documentation. Our tools will scan these areas, but our technicians will not.

Is it secure?

Very. We use Teamviewer version 9 to allow us to connect to your PC. Teamviewer uses military grade encryption to establish a two-way connection. We also are unable to reconnect to your PC in the future once our services have been completed.

What forms of payment to you accept?

VISA, Mastercard, Discover, PayPal.

Will someone named Habeeb from India be working with me?

Nope, just folks from our shop here in Illinois.


I have dial-up internet service, can you help me?

Unforunatley No. We will need to connect over a broadband / high speed connection.

 

Please feel free to contact us if you have any other questions?
