Remote Support



What to expect

After clicking on the Remote Support button, your computer will begin to download the software that will be used to allow your technician to connect to your PC. Your Cloud PC Repair technician will walk you through these easy steps.


Once connected your technician will begin to repair your PC. You will have the option to speak with your tech via PC Chat, or call them at anytime during the repair process. Your technician will also keep you up-to-date during the enire process if you like.
